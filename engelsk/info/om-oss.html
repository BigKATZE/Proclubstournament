About us

Who's Proclubstournament.com?

Proclubstournaments.no is a newly created ... that organises proclub tournaments on EA FC 24. We aim to create a great experience for everyone who participates in our tournaments, and we hope that you will join us on our journey.

We have capacity for 96 teams, 48 for Old Gen (PS4/Xbox One) and 48 New Gen (PS5 & Xbox Series X), but we also have a future desire to expand the number of possible layers.


How do the tournaments work?

All communication takes place on our Discord server, link here, so to participate in our tournaments you need to be a member of our discord server.

As of today, we have 16 groups, 8 for Old Gen and 8 for New Gen, with 6 teams per group. Everyone plays 1 match against each other, and the 1-3 go on to the a-playoffs, while place 4 goes to the b-playoffs and places 5 and 6 are out of the tournament.
